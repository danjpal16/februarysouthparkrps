How to Play

Hi there, my name is Stan Marsh and today I will teach you how to play rock, papper, scissors:

  1. Choose either rock, paper, or scissors
  2. Rock beats Scissors
  3. Scissors beats Paper
  4. Paper beats Rock
  5. Each round you win you get a point
  6. Each round you lose you don't get a point
  7. First to five points wins
